About LeatherdbyB

We are a premium leather goods company specializing in handcrafted products made from the finest materials. Our mission is to combine traditional craftsmanship with modern design to create timeless pieces that last.

Our Story

Founded in 2015 in Lagos, Nigeria, LeatherdbyB started as a small workshop crafting bespoke leather goods for local clients. Our commitment to quality and attention to detail quickly gained recognition, and we've since grown into a respected brand known for our premium leather products across Africa.

Our Craftsmanship

Each LeatherdbyB product is meticulously handcrafted by our skilled artisans using traditional techniques passed down through generations. We source only the finest full-grain leathers and use natural vegetable tanning processes to ensure durability and a beautiful patina that develops over time.
