Care Guide

Preserve the elegance and durability of your leather goods with our expert care tips.

1. Regular Cleaning

Wipe down your leather products using a soft, dry or slightly damp cloth. Avoid soaking the leather or using harsh cleaning products.

2. Conditioning

Use a high-quality leather conditioner every few months to keep the leather moisturized and prevent cracking or drying out.

3. Avoid Water

Leather is not waterproof. If it gets wet, blot the water with a soft cloth and allow it to air dry naturallyâ€”away from direct heat or sunlight.

4. Storage

Store leather goods in a cool, dry place. Use a dust bag for handbags and avoid hanging them to retain their shape.

5. Handle with Clean Hands

Natural oils and dirt from your skin can stain leather over time. Always handle your leather items with clean hands.
